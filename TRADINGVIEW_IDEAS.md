**Symbol:** NSE:BANKNIFTY
**Timeframe:** 15m
**Direction:** Neutral

**Content:**
```
🎯 Support & Resistance - The Right Way

Most traders draw S/R wrong. Here's my method:

❌ Wrong:
- Single thin lines
- Too many levels
- Random placement

✅ Right:
- ZONES not lines (price is messy)
- Max 2-3 key levels
- Based on REACTIONS not just highs/lows

How to Find Strong Zones:

1. Look for CLUSTERS of rejection
   - 3+ touches = strong zone
   - Recent touches = more relevant

2. Check VOLUME at level
   - High volume rejection = strong
   - Low volume = weak, likely to break

3. Mark as ZONE not line
   - Usually 0.3-0.5% width
   - Price respects areas, not exact numbers

Today's Key Zones (BANKNIFTY):
📗 Support: 48,200-48,350
📕 Resistance: 48,800-48,950

Trade Plan:
- Long near support with SL below zone

**Symbol:** NSE:NIFTY
**Timeframe:** Daily
**Direction:** Neutral (Educational)

**Content:**
```
💰 The #1 Skill Nobody Talks About

You can have:
- Perfect entries
- Great analysis
- Right direction

And STILL blow up your account.

Why? Position sizing.

My Risk Rules:

1️⃣ Never risk more than 2% per trade
   Account: ₹1,00,000
   Max risk: ₹2,000 per trade

2️⃣ Calculate position BEFORE entry
   Entry: 22,500
   Stop Loss: 22,400 (100 points)
   Max loss: ₹2,000
   Position: ₹2,000/100 = 20 qty

3️⃣ Risk:Reward minimum 1:2
   Risking ₹2,000?
   Target minimum: ₹4,000 profit

4️⃣ Daily loss limit: 5%
   Lost 5%? Stop trading for the day.
   No revenge trades.

5️⃣ Weekly loss limit: 10%
   Take a break. Review what went wrong.

The Math:
- 50% win rate + 1:2 RR = profitable
- 10 trades: 5 losses (₹10k) vs 5 wins (₹20k)
- Net: +₹10,000

You don't need to be right most of the time.
You need to lose SMALL and win BIG.
