## Reply Guidelines

### DO:
- Add genuine insight or perspective
- Ask thoughtful follow-up questions
- Share relevant data or examples
- Be helpful to other readers

### DON'T:
- Mention PaperPe directly (build credibility first)
- Be promotional or salesy
- Copy-paste generic replies
- Reply to everything (quality > quantity)

### Ideal Reply Format:
```
[Insight or agreement] + [Value-add or question]

Example:
"This is so true about position sizing! I've seen traders with 90% win rate blow up because they sized up after a winning streak. The 2% rule exists for a reason."
```
