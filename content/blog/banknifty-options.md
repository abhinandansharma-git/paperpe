# BANKNIFTY Options Trading - Complete Strategy Guide

*Updated: March 2026 | Reading time: 9 min*

**BANKNIFTY is the most traded index option in India.** Higher volatility means bigger profits (and losses). Heres how to trade it smartly.

## Why Trade BANKNIFTY?

- **Higher volatility** than NIFTY
- **Banking sector focus** - 12 major banks
- **Better percentage moves** - More opportunity
- **High liquidity** - Easy entry/exit

## BANKNIFTY vs NIFTY Options

| Factor | BANKNIFTY | NIFTY |
|--------|-----------|-------|
| Volatility | Higher | Lower |
| Premium | Higher | Lower |
| Moves | 200-500 pts/day | 100-200 pts/day |
| Risk | Higher | Lower |
| Best For | Experienced | Beginners |

## Best BANKNIFTY Trading Times

- **9:15-9:30** - Opening volatility
- **9:45-10:30** - Trend establishment
- **2:30-3:15** - Power hour
- **Avoid** - 12:00-1:30 PM (low volume)

## BANKNIFTY Strategies

### 1. Directional (CE/PE Buying)
- Buy CE for bullish view
- Buy PE for bearish view
- Use ATM or 1 strike OTM
- SL: 30% of premium

### 2. Straddle/Strangle
- For high volatility days
- Buy both CE and PE
- Profit from big moves

### 3. Credit Spreads
- Sell OTM, buy further OTM
- Defined risk
- Profit from time decay

## Risk Management

1. **Max 2-3 trades per day**
2. **Never trade without SL**
3. **Position size: 1-2 lots max**
4. **Book partial profits at 50%**
5. **Exit by 3:15 PM**
