# Virtual Trading in India - How to Practice Without Risk

*Updated: March 2026 | Reading time: 6 min*

**90% of traders lose money in their first year.** Virtual trading (paper trading) helps you learn without risking real capital.

## What is Virtual Trading?

Virtual trading simulates real market conditions using fake money. You can:
- Practice buy/sell orders
- Test trading strategies
- Learn platform mechanics
- Build confidence

## Benefits of Virtual Trading

1. **Zero Financial Risk** - Lose virtual money, not real money
2. **Real Market Conditions** - Live prices, real volatility
3. **Strategy Testing** - Validate before going live
4. **Emotional Training** - Learn to handle wins/losses
5. **Platform Familiarity** - Master order types, charts

## How to Practice Effectively

1. **Set realistic capital** - Rs 1-5 lakh
2. **Trade regular hours** - 9:15 AM - 3:30 PM
3. **Follow strict rules** - Same as real trading
4. **Keep a journal** - Track every trade
5. **Review weekly** - Analyze performance

## When to Go Live?

You are ready when:
- 100+ paper trades completed
- Profitable for 30 consecutive days
- Win rate above 50%
- Max drawdown controlled
- Emotions managed
