## What is Paper Trading?

Paper trading (also called virtual trading or simulated trading) lets you practice buying and selling stocks, options, and commodities using virtual money. Its the safest way to learn trading before putting real capital at risk.

## Why Paper Trade First?

- **Zero Risk**: Learn without losing money
- **Build Confidence**: Test strategies in real market conditions
- **Understand Platforms**: Get familiar with order types, charts, and tools
- **Track Performance**: See your win rate before going live

## How to Start Paper Trading

1. **Choose a platform** - PaperPe is free and India-focused
2. **Set virtual capital** - Start with realistic amount (Rs 1-5 lakh)
3. **Trade like its real** - Follow your rules strictly
4. **Track everything** - Journal every trade
5. **Review weekly** - Analyze what worked and what didnt

## Common Paper Trading Mistakes

1. **Not taking it seriously** - Trade like its real money
2. **Oversized positions** - Use realistic position sizes
3. **Skipping stop losses** - Always set SL
4. **Not tracking** - Keep a trading journal
