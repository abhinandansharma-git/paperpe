# MCX Trading for Beginners - Complete Guide 2026

*Updated: March 2026 | Reading time: 8 min*

**Want to trade commodities in India?** MCX (Multi Commodity Exchange) lets you trade Gold, Silver, Crude Oil, and more. Heres your complete guide.

## What is MCX?

MCX is Indias largest commodity derivatives exchange. You can trade:
- **Metals**: Gold, Silver, Copper, Aluminum
- **Energy**: Crude Oil, Natural Gas
- **Agri**: Cotton, Mentha Oil

## MCX Trading Hours

| Session | Time (IST) |
|---------|------------|
| Morning | 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM |
| Evening | 5:00 PM - 11:30 PM |

*Note: Evening session timing changes with US daylight saving*

## Most Popular MCX Contracts

### 1. CRUDEOIL
- Most liquid commodity
- Follows international crude prices
- High volatility = high opportunity

### 2. GOLD
- Safe haven asset
- Moves opposite to equity markets
- Good for hedging

### 3. SILVER
- More volatile than gold
- Industrial + precious metal
- Higher percentage moves

## How to Start MCX Trading

1. **Paper trade first** - [Practice on PaperPe](/dashboard)
2. **Open commodity account** - Zerodha, Angel One, Dhan
3. **Start with mini lots** - Lower capital requirement
4. **Trade one commodity** - Master it before diversifying

## MCX Trading Strategies

### Trend Following
- Trade in direction of larger trend
- Use moving averages (20, 50 EMA)
- Enter on pullbacks

### News-Based Trading
- US inventory data (Wed/Thu)
- OPEC meetings
- Fed decisions

### Range Trading
- Identify support/resistance
- Buy at support, sell at resistance
- Works in sideways markets

## Risk Management

- **Max 2% per trade** - Never risk more
- **Stop loss mandatory** - Always set SL
- **Position sizing** - Calculate before entry
