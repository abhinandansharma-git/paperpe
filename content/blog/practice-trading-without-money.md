# How to Practice Trading Without Money in India

*Updated: March 2026 | Reading time: 5 min*

**Want to learn trading but dont want to lose money?** Paper trading lets you practice with virtual capital. Heres how to start.

## The Problem

- Real trading needs real money
- Beginners lose money learning
- Expensive education
- No way to test strategies safely

## The Solution: Paper Trading

Paper trading = Virtual trading = Demo trading

You get:
- Fake money (Rs 1-10 lakh)
- Real market prices
- Practice buy/sell
- Track performance

## Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Choose Platform

PaperPe - Free, India-focused
[Start here](/dashboard)

### Step 2: Set Virtual Capital

Start with what you would really invest:
- Rs 50,000 - Small account
- Rs 1,00,000 - Medium account
- Rs 5,00,000 - Large account

### Step 3: Learn Basics

- How to place orders
- Market vs Limit orders
- Stop loss orders
- Reading charts

### Step 4: Start Trading

- Trade during market hours
- Start with 1-2 trades per day
- Keep position sizes small
- Always use stop loss

### Step 5: Track Everything

- Entry price
- Exit price
- Profit/Loss
- Why you took the trade
- What you learned

### Step 6: Review Weekly

- What worked?
- What failed?
- Patterns in wins/losses?
- How to improve?

## Common Mistakes

1. Not taking it seriously
2. Skipping stop losses
3. Not keeping a journal
4. Trading too much
5. Not reviewing performance
