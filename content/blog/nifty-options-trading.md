## What are NIFTY Options?

NIFTY options are derivatives contracts based on the NIFTY 50 index. They give you the right (not obligation) to buy or sell NIFTY at a specific price before expiry.

## Types of NIFTY Options

### Call Options (CE)
- Profit when NIFTY goes UP
- Buy CE when bullish
- Example: NIFTY 22000 CE

### Put Options (PE)
- Profit when NIFTY goes DOWN
- Buy PE when bearish
- Example: NIFTY 22000 PE

## Key Terms to Know

| Term | Meaning |
|------|--------|
| Strike Price | The price at which you can buy/sell |
| Premium | Cost of buying the option |
| Expiry | When the option expires (every Thursday) |
| ITM | In The Money - profitable |
| OTM | Out of The Money - not yet profitable |
| ATM | At The Money - strike = current price |

## NIFTY Options Trading Strategies

### 1. Buying Calls (Bullish)
- Simple directional bet
- Limited risk (premium paid)
- Unlimited profit potential

### 2. Buying Puts (Bearish)
- Bet on market falling
- Limited risk
- Great for hedging

### 3. Straddle (Neutral)
- Buy both CE and PE at same strike
- Profit from big moves either direction
- High cost strategy

### 4. Iron Condor (Range)
- Sell OTM options on both sides
- Profit when market stays in range
- Defined risk

## Common Mistakes

1. **Buying OTM options** - High probability of loss
2. **No stop loss** - Can lose entire premium
3. **Expiry day trading** - Extremely volatile
4. **Overtrading** - 2-3 quality trades per day is enough

## Best Time to Trade NIFTY Options

- **9:30-10:30 AM** - Morning momentum
- **2:00-3:15 PM** - Power hour moves
- **Avoid** - 11:00 AM - 1:00 PM (lunch chop)
